SmallAppeal.org

2019 - Present

SmallAppeal is an NGO based in Bangalore working on community education and food security. SmallAppeal was born to adopt an ageing Govt. Primary School. The school was built by the British in 1919 as a community school. After independence of India, the school was converted to a government run school. The school was left in disaray and on the verge of collapse. The school is important in the community which is under priveleged. To keep the school running, SmallAppeal was created and hence adopted the school to run it.
